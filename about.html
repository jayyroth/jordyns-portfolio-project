About Me

I am a Florida Native, born & raised in Clearwater. Recently I moved to Kansas City, Missouri with my partner and our cat Binx. I am a University of Central Florida alumni, where I studied both Event & Hospitality Management at the Rosen College of Hospitality Management. My passion for events started with music festivals, and remains so. I've had several incredible opportunities to work festivals all over the country, including Electric Daisy Carnival in Las Vegas, the largest music festival in North America.
